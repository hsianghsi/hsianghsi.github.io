Hsiang Hsi Lu

Hsiang Hsi Lu is a researcher, photographer, and urban analyst who splits his time between New York and Taipei. His work focuses on data-driven urbanism, analyzing cities, built environment, and spatial trends. He has exhibited his work at the Carriage Trade Gallery, SVA Flatiron Gallery, and others. He holds a Master of Science in Computational Design Practices from Columbia University and a Bachelor of Science in Industrial Design from National Cheng Kung University.
