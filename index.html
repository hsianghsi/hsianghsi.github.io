Hsiang Hsi Lu

Hsiang Hsi Lu, a researcher, artist, and urban analyst, splits his time between New York and Taipei. His work centers on data-driven urbanism by analyzing cities, policy, and spatial trends. His work has been exhibited at the Carriage Trade Gallery, SVA Flatiron Gallery, and others. He holds a Master of Science in Computational Design Practices from Columbia University and a Bachelor of Science in Industrial Design from National Cheng Kung University.
